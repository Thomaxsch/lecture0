This is Abbie

Details

Abbie was the result of a brief relationship between Edwina and Abe during the Second World War. Abe was in England before D-Day and had one night of passion with Edwina before he had to leave England. Unknown to Abe, Edwina became pregnant and the result was Abbie. Abe and the rest of the Simpson family first met Abbie when she was 58 years old.
